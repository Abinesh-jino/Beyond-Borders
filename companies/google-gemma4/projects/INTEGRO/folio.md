## Project Summary

_Folio is an AI-powered learning companion that transforms any piece of content into retained knowledge.
Users can scan textbooks, research papers, articles, handwritten notes, PDFs, and images using their camera or file uploads. Folio instantly performs OCR, summarizes the content, and translates it into the user's preferred language—all in a single action.
Unlike traditional OCR or note-taking applications, Folio continuously builds a personalized learning system. Every scan is stored as a dated learning entry, enabling AI-generated weekly and monthly learning recaps. Over time, Folio creates a personal knowledge graph that automatically links concepts across subjects, helping users discover relationships between ideas and improve long-term retention.
Powered by Gemma 4B, Folio aims to make learning faster, more accessible, and easier to remember._

---

## Motivation

_Today's learners face three major challenges:
Information is abundant but difficult to retain.
Learning workflows are fragmented across multiple tools for scanning, translation, summarization, and note-taking.
Knowledge remains isolated, making it difficult to connect concepts over time.
Research shows that learners forget a significant portion of newly acquired information within days if it is not revisited. Existing tools focus on content consumption rather than knowledge retention.
Folio bridges this gap by combining content capture, understanding, retention, and knowledge connection into a single AI-powered learning platform._

---

## Goals

- Improve long-term retention through periodic AI-generated learning recaps.
- Reduce language barriers to educational content.
- Make learning more interactive, searchable, and memorable.
